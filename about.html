About me

Hey there! I'm Erika. A mother of four amazing children, married to my very best friend, a perpetual optimist and a photographer in Susquehanna, PA. I specialize in portrait photography.

I am humbled every year by the wonderful people who invite me to document their lives! I have proclaimed for almost a decade that I have the "Best job EVER!" and I stand by that- I am smitten with my profession, truly. I enjoy all types of sessions from newborns, families, seniors, couples, engagements and boudoir- they all bring me a unique challenge and so much reward.

When I'm not behind the camera you can find me volunteering somewhere in the community, cheering on one of my kids in their activities or spending way too much money at Homegoods! As my kids get older I've been enjoying having more time to dream up new ideas for fun sets and mini session events. I look forward to working with you!
